Shugar less malai kulfi

Description

Ingredients 3 lb whole milk 2 can condensed milk as I showed 2 container evaporated milk

Cooking

1

Boil the milk and stir it so it doesn't burn the base Boil or stir the milk until it becomes one third of whole

2

Now let it cool little bit and add condensed sweet milk Freeze it and you can put in molds to give some shapes

3

Yummy delicious sugar less malai kulfi is ready
